AboutMe

WebDeveloper!

I'm Hazel Jane Valencia from Sindangan, Zamboanga del Norte. While pursuing criminology to serve justice, I channel my analytical mindset into web development—building sites for school projects and exploring tech solutions for crime prevention. Whether it's code or criminal psychology, I believe in using skills to make a difference.

As a criminology student, I am deeply committed to understanding how law enforcement and technology can work hand in hand. I believe that digital tools like data analytics, surveillance systems, and cybersecurity can play a major role in preventing and solving crimes. By learning both criminology and web development, I aim to contribute to a safer society through innovative, tech-based solutions in the field of public safety and criminal justice.

My long-term goal is to become a policewoman who is not only physically and mentally prepared for duty, but also equipped with digital knowledge to solve modern problems. By combining discipline, empathy, and technical expertise, I want to serve the community in a smarter and more effective way.
